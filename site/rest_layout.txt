/articles							?page=#-#&format=json					list all articles (index page, format defaults to html)
/articles/<int:id> 					?format=json							view single article (article page, format defaults to html)
/articles/<int:id>/annotations 		?										list annotations for article (defaults to json)
/annotations						?										list all annotations (defaults to json)
/annotations/<int:id>				?										view single annotation (defaults to json)
