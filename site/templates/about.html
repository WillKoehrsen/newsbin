welcome to newbin

This site fetches articles from a lot of different news sources and tries to annotate the articles with information about the people and places being discussed. What this means is that you can click on names in the articles and get immediate information in a small window.

On the front page you can select your sources, the number of results to return or search titles and content with vanilla or regex searches. Clicking any of the titles will take you to the article itself.

If you see an annotation that's wrong, copy and paste the highlighted text into the input bar at the top of the article, flip the switch to 'remove' and submit. If you want to add an annotation that doesn't exist, copy text into the input bar, flip to 'add' and hit the go button.
