welcome to newbin

This site fetches articles from a lot of different news sources, detects proper names in them, and tries to annotate the articles with information about the people and places being discusses. What this means is that you can click on names in the news and get immediate information about them in a sidebar.

If you see an annotation that is wrong, scroll to the bottom of the article and click the red minus-sign next to it. If you see an annotation that should be included, highlight it in the text and click yes on the notification.

You can select your sources, the number of results to return or search titles and content with plain string or regex searches from the front page.
Enjoy.
