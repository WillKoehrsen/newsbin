welcome to newbin

This site fetches articles from a lot of different news sources, detects proper names in them, and tries to annotate the articles with information about the people mentioned in them. What this means is that you can click on names in the news and get a summary and other information about them.

If you see an annotation that is wrong, scroll to the bottom of the article and click the red minus-sign next to it. If you think one is missing, highlight it in the text, and click the green plus-sign to add it.

You can select your sources, the number of results to return or search titles and content with plain string or regex searches from the front page. You can also view a log that dumps notifications from the engine as it fetches and parses articles. Enjoy.
